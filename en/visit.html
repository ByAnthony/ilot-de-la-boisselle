îlot La Boisselle What to expect

Discovering
the battlefield

Accompanied by a guide, explore this unique battlefield to learn more about its history and the soldiers who fought there. You will walk through this site of more than two hectares, around its craters and vestiges of the trench warfare.

Guided Tour

A Battlefield tour takes approximately 1h30 and this is a wonderful way to learn more about the site with an opportunity to ask questions. We invit you to book in advance for a specific date. Please be aware to wear a good pair of boots. Toilets are available on site.
